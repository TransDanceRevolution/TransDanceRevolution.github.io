## Focuses

- House Shuffle
- Heel Toe + Variations + Happy Feet

## Steps

1. House Shuffle x2 - to right first
2. Toe Tap Combo - left foot tap behind right foot, ends with 2 happy feet, take this part slow
3. Heel Toe Combo - right foot first, ends with 2 happy feet, take this part fast
4. Running Man x4
5. Scoop and stall on right foot
6. Heel toe twist variation - ends with happy feet
7. Running man x 4
8. Scoop right to spin - this should happen at around 0:13
9. W Step - Freestyle however many feel right
