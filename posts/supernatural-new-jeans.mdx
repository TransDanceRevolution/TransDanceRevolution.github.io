# Steps

1. Crab Walk x4 - back and forth starting toes pointed together heels apart, see linked video above
2. Bedrock x4 - starting on right foot
3. ATL Stomp x4 - starting on right foot kicking out
4. Running Man x8 - starting on right foot
5. Leg Hook - hopping on to left leg, then kicking out your right leg and placing it over your left
6. Shuffle Stomp - right foot down and kicking left foot back
7. Kick - right foot kick, hopping onto left foot again after swinging it back and then to the center again
8. Grapevine - starts with right foot weaving behind left foot
