## Key Points

- Jogging In Place - Timing
- Shuffle Stomp

## Steps

1. Polly Pocket x4 - turning to right to show right inner thigh on last step
2. Run in Place x2 - 2 steps total
3. Shuffle Stomp x2 - Make sure to do this on the same foot
4. Running Man x4 turn to right to face front
5. W-Step x1
6. Charleston x1 - backwards
7. Running Man x4 - slide backwards on last
