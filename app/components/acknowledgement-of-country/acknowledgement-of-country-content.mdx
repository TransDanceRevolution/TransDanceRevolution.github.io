Trans Dance Revolution **recognises the traditional custodians of the land in which we play on.**

We all bleed under colonialism and capitalism so it is imperative we recognise that **no one is free until we are all free.**

First Nations peoples are disproportionately impoverished and incarcerated on this continent and will continue to be without systemic and structural change.

**Their sovereignty hasn't been ceded and we pay our respects to elders past, present and emerging.**

**This always was and always will be aboriginal land.**
